About Biotechpro

Biotechpro is a leading producer of high-quality wood chips in Moldova. We are committed to sustainability and environmental responsibility. Our wood chips are made from carefully selected wood sources and undergo a rigorous production process to ensure optimal quality. We serve various industries, including biomass energy, agriculture, and landscaping. With our expertise and dedication, we aim to contribute to a greener and more sustainable future.
